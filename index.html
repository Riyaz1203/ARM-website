MANAGING DIRECTOR

A Managing Director, or Management Director, is responsible for upholding successful company operations by implementing business strategies and fostering business relationships. Their duties include maintaining frequent communications with company Executives and Board Members, reviewing operational data and financial statements to track the progress of business initiatives and hiring Department Directors to guide company employees in areas like accounting, sales, marketing or HR.

As the leader of the management team, the MD motivates and oversees the executive staff, managers, and wider employee base. Common tasks include workforce planning, talent recruitment and development, delegating responsibilities, performance evaluations, and maintaining workplace.
